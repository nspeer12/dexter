Gestures and You

Hands-free communication with computers has long been a science fiction dream. But with budding technology and innovative drive, gesture based commands are no longer a thing of the future, but the present. Gestures are a nonverbal way to manipulate your desktop to work for you when your hands are otherwise tied. They are a core part of Dexter's framework and something we wanted to be completely unique to each user.
