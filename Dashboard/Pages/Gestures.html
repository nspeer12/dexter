Gestures and You

Hands-free communication with computers has long been a science fiction dream. But with budding technology and innovative drive, gesture based commands are no longer a thing of the future, but the present. Gestures are a nonverbal way to manipulate your desktop to work for you when your hands are otherwise tied. They are a core part of Dexter's framework and something we wanted to be completely unique to each user. Gestures are recorded via webcam, where hand-tracking technology memorizes the user's hand motions and transforms them into readable, useable commands capable of doing anything a mouse and keyboard could do.
